Iain Haughton

Lead Research Scientist at the Dyson Robot Learning Lab, where I am part of a team focused on data-driven robot learning.

At Dyson, I have contributed to all aspects of robotics, including mechanical design, control systems, electronics, sensors, computer vision, and machine learning. My expertise in AI was further honed during a two-year secondment to Imperial College, where I collaborated closely with Andrew Davison and his team.

Recently, I spearheaded the initiative to establish the Dyson Robot Learning Lab, focussing on pioneering robot learning technologies.
